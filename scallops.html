Scallops

This is how you make a pan seared scallops

You will go insane once you try these scallops.

Ingredients

  • fresh scallops
  • butter
  • garlic
  • parsly
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat the scallops dry.
  2. Season the scallops
  3. Melt butter in pan. Add chopped garlic. Cook scallops until seared on each side.
